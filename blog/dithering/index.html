dithering and age of empires 2

written 13 February 2025

oooo dithering ooooo! it makes things look really pretty. I think i have a laptop sticker somewhere that's dithered clouds or something and it looks real nice. Check out this tool for dithering: https://doodad.dev/dither-me-this/ . Ordered dithering with mat height 2 or 4 looks great. the Commodore pallet is great. b and w is fine.

Also aoe2 is really funny for having a soundboard with taunts that get played globally when you type in numbers. I've been using number 7 "aaaaaaa." pretty often. In a game once where I did something stupid with monks, the enemy team bm'd me with "What is your strategy?" "Attack the enemy with monks." Had me on the floor.

Some honorable mentions are dadgum (sounds like "Dad? Gone!") and the iconic wololo.

idialedyournumber sounds really nice. Found them from Bunny Goes 2 Business School on youtube.
